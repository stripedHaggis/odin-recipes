Prawn Linguine Recipe

Ingredients

  • Prawns
  • Linguine
  • White Wine
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Chorizo
  • Mushroom
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil Linguine in pan of salty water. Drain and set aside when cooked, keeping a small amount of pasta water.
  2. Fry chorizo at high heat with a small amount of olive oil.
  3. When cooked remove chorizo from pan. Turn the heat down to medium/low.
  4. Add crushed garlic and butter to the pan.
  5. Fry prawns in garlic butter for a couple of minutes. Remove when cooked.
  6. Add mushrooms to the pan and fry for 2 mins.
  7. Add wine to the pan and allow to reduce.
  8. Stir in prawns and chorizo.
  9. Add cooked linguine and the remaining butter. Stir to combine.
